Next, we move the negation through the universal quantification: There exists a natural number nnn such that there exists no natural number mmm such that nnn is not less than or equal to m.m\text{.}m.

We continue propagating the negation: There exists a natural number nnn such that for all natural numbers mmm we do not have that nnn is not less than or equal to m.m\text{.}m.

We can now eliminate the double negation: There exists a natural number nnn such that for all natural numbers mmm we have that nnn is less than or equal to m.m\text{.}m.
